Frequently asked questions

If you can't find what you're looking for, send us an email to: info@open-source-advice.com with your enquiry.

General Questions

What is open-source-advice.com?
open-source-advice.com is a platform where developers can find and seek advice from the open source community.
How does it work?
People who are involved in open source can create a service offer which is then visible to everyone else. Other developers who need advice can then contact them.
How does Payment work?
We don't offer payment possibilites and therefore encourage you to negotiate yourself. We only provide a platform where people can connect. This also means that our platform does not take any money.
How does communication work?
Help requests are always sent over Email. We therefore encourage everyone to regularily check their emails. On our plattform there is no messaging sending possible.
Who is behind open-source-advice.com?
I am a solo developer trying to build usefull things. Check out the about section for more info

For open source maintainer / contributor / experts

Why should I participate?
Monetization, Marketing & future Contributors: For most of the Open Source projects monetization is a big problem. By providing dedicated consulting this can help finance parts of the project. Additionally you promote your project and help others with their problem. Maybe you can find a future contributor ;)
Can I offer advice?
Most of the people who give advice are either maintainer or contributor of a project. If you are none of these you can still offer advice as an expert.
How do you know that I am a maintainer, contributor or expert?
We don't. Please be honest in terms of your work and experience.
Do I need to check open-source-advice.com on a regular basis?
No you don't as the communication is purerly done over email. You only need to sign in if you want to make account or service changes.

For developers seeking help

Why should I use open-source-advice.com?
With open-source-advice.com you get help from people who actively contributed to a project. Who else could be better to ask? ;)
I am looking for help. What do I need to do?
Search on our main page for open source projects. If you found someone then you need to register for an account. After that you can send a message. Please describe your inquery as precise as possible. Both of you will receive an email.
The project I am looking for is not available. What can I do?
We are sorry to hear that. As a very young platform we are constantly trying to onboard new projects. Feel free to drop an email to: info@open-source-advice.com and we try to get in contact with the project.
